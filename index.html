Introduction

Welcome to the Art Exhibition for 2021. This year, all the artworks were made by children from the age group of 13-16-year-olds. But unfortunately due to the current scenario (The Pandemic: Due to Covid-19), we didn't get many participants. We kept the topic open-ended this year. We thought having a topic can restrain the children from making something truly exceptional and we have received some amazing results. We hope you find the variety of artworks fascinating and we hope you enjoy it.
